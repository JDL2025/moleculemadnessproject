🧪 Molecule Madness!

Choose your molecules · Build them with gumdrops & toothpicks · Show the world their shapes!

🍬 How to Build Your Molecule

1Gumdrops = Atoms.
Pick a color to represent each element the molecule is made of. You have up to 5 colors!
2Toothpicks = Bonds.
Bonds are the connections between atoms.
3Double Bonds = TWO toothpicks.
Stronger than single bonds. Use two toothpicks side by side to show double bonds.

Project Part 2

Share Your Molecule!

You built it — now share it! Post your best model to your class Padlet.

📋 Project Part 2: Follow This Workflow

🎨
Step 1
Make Your Color Legend
Use a notecard to make a color legend that tells people what color gumdrops represent what elements.
📸
Step 2
Take Your Photo
Take a clear, well-lit photo of your most complex assembled molecule with your color legend visible in the shot!
🔘
Step 3
Tap Your Class Padlet
Scroll down and find your science class period below. Tap your button to open your class Padlet.
⬆️
Step 4
Upload Your Photo
Create a new post on the Padlet. Upload your photo and you are required to post using your REAL FIRST AND LAST NAME.
✍️
Step 5
Add Your Molecule Info
In your post, include your molecule's name and at least two fun facts about it. What makes it cool? What does it do in the real world?
👀
Step 6
Explore & Comment
Browse your classmates' posts and leave at least 3 positive comments on other people's molecules. Be specific — what do you like about their build?
🔬
Step 7
Ask a Science Question
Find at least one classmate's post and reply with a genuine scientific question about their molecule. Get curious!
🎨
Photo tip: Place your color legend next to your model before photographing. Without it, viewers won't know which gumdrop color represents which element — and that's half the science!
✅
Checklist before you're done: Photo posted ✦ Real full name on post ✦ Molecule name + 2 fun facts written ✦ 3 positive comments left ✦ 1 scientific question asked
